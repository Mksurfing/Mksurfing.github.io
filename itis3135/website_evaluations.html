Website Evaluations

Arngren.net

Arngren.net is a cluttered, outdated website where the layout is chaotic, colors clash, and alignment is non-existent.

  • Contrast: The colors blend together, making text hard to read.
  • Repetition: No consistent use of fonts, colors, or design patterns.
  • Alignment: Items are randomly placed with no structure.
  • Proximity: Related items are not grouped, making it confusing to navigate.

Assessment: This site does not follow any of the CRAP principles well, resulting in a visually confusing experience.

Unseen.co

Unseen.co is a clean, modern website with clear layout and visual hierarchy that guides the user efficiently.

  • Contrast: Text and backgrounds are easy to read with clear color differences.
  • Repetition: Consistent use of fonts, colors, and spacing throughout the site.
  • Alignment: Elements are aligned neatly to create structure.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped together logically.

Assessment: This site follows the CRAP principles well, making it easy to navigate and visually appealing.
