My hobby revolves around exploring the depth of indie games, appreciating their music, world-building, and hand-drawn art.

How: Getting Started

Start small, pick a game that speaks to you. Whether it's a platformer like Celeste or a Metroidvania like Hollow Knight, what matters most is finding what inspires you to keep playing.

All you need is a device (PC, console, or even a phone), a bit of patience, and interest for games!
